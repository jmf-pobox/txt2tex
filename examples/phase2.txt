=== Equivalence Chains ===

** Solution 1 **

(a) Commutative property

EQUIV:
p and q
q and p [commutative]

(b) De Morgan law

EQUIV:
not (p and q)
(not p) or (not q) [De Morgan]
not p or not q [parentheses]

** Solution 2 **

(a) Definition of implication

EQUIV:
p => q
not p or q [definition]

(b) Multiple equivalence steps

EQUIV:
(p and q) or (p and r)
p and (q or r) [distributive]
(q or r) and p [commutative]
p and (r or q) [commutative]

** Solution 3 **

(a) Equivalence chain without justifications

EQUIV:
p or p
p

(b) Mixed justifications

EQUIV:
p <=> q
(p => q) and (q => p) [definition]
(not p or q) and (not q or p)
