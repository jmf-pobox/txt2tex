=== Phase 11d: Lambda Expressions ===

TEXT: Lambda expressions provide a concise way to define anonymous functions.

(a)
TEXT: Simple lambda expression

lambda x : N . x^2

(b)
TEXT: Multi-variable lambda

lambda x, y : N . x and y

(c)
TEXT: Lambda with comparison

lambda x : N . x > 0

(d)
TEXT: Nested lambda expression

lambda x : X . lambda y : Y . x and y

(e)
TEXT: Lambda with quantifier in body

lambda x : X . forall y : Y | x or y
