=== Equivalence Chains ===

** Solution 1 **

(a) Commutative property

EQUIV:
p land q
q land p [commutative]

(b) De Morgan law

EQUIV:
lnot (p land q)
(lnot p) lor (lnot q) [De Morgan]
lnot p lor lnot q [parentheses]

** Solution 2 **

(a) Definition of implication

EQUIV:
p => q
lnot p lor q [definition]

(b) Multiple equivalence steps

EQUIV:
(p land q) lor (p land r)
p land (q lor r) [distributive]
(q lor r) land p [commutative]
p land (r lor q) [commutative]

** Solution 3 **

(a) Equivalence chain without justifications

EQUIV:
p lor p
p

(b) Mixed justifications

EQUIV:
p <=> q
(p => q) land (q => p) [definition]
(lnot p lor q) land (lnot q lor p)
