=== Propositional Logic ===

** Solution 1 **

(a) p and q => p

(b) TRUTH TABLE:
p | q | p => q

(c) not (p and q) <=> (not p) or (not q)

** Solution 2 **

(a) (p or q) and r <=> (p and r) or (q and r)
