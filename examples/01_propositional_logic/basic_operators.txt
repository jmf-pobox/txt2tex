=== Propositional Logic ===

** Solution 1 **

(a) p land q => p

(b) TRUTH TABLE:
p | q | p => q
T | T | T
T | F | F
F | T | T
F | F | T

(c) lnot (p land q) <=> (lnot p) lor (lnot q)

** Solution 2 **

(a) (p lor q) land r <=> (p land r) lor (q land r)

(b) TRUTH TABLE:
p | q | p <=> q
T | T | T
T | F | F
F | T | F
F | F | T

(c) p => q => r
