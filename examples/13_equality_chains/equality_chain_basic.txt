=== Equality Chains (EQUAL: block) ===

** Example 1: Base case - length of the empty sequence **

(a) Prove length <> = 0.

EQUAL:
length <>
0 [by definition of length]

** Example 2: Inductive step - length of a non-empty sequence **

(a) Prove length (s ^ <x>) = length s + 1.

EQUAL:
length (s ^ <x>)
length s + 1 [by definition of length and concatenation]

** Example 3: Multi-step chain with justifications **

EQUAL:
length (s ^ t)
length s + length t [by length-concatenation lemma]
length t + length s [commutativity of +]
