=== Sequence Basics ===

** Example 1: Sequence Types **

TEXT: Sequence types define collections of ordered elements:

seq N

seq Z

iseq N

** Example 2: Sequence Literals (Unicode) **

TEXT: Sequences can be written with Unicode angle brackets:

⟨⟩

⟨a⟩

⟨a, b, c⟩

⟨1, 2, 3, 4⟩

** Example 3: Sequence Literals (ASCII) **

TEXT: ASCII angle brackets are also supported:

<>

<a>

<a, b, c>

<1, 2, 3, 4>

** Example 4: Sequences in Expressions **

TEXT: Sequences can appear in various contexts:

s in seq N

# <>

# <a, b, c, d>
