TEXT: This is a simple example and demonstration of the migration tool.
TEXT: The system should not replace English words in prose text.

// Mathematical expression - should be migrated
p land q => p

TRUTH TABLE:
p | q | p land q | p lor q | lnot p
T | T | T       | T      | F
T | F | F       | T      | F
F | T | F       | T      | T
F | F | F       | F      | T

EQUIV:
p land (q lor r)
<=> (p land q) lor (p land r) [distributivity]
<=> lnot (lnot p lor (lnot q land lnot r)) [De Morgan]

TEXT: The formula p or q is a disjunction, and the formula lnot p is a negation.
TEXT: Both formulas are important concepts in propositional logic.

axdef
  validPassword : P String
where
  forall pw : String |
    pw in validPassword <=>
      # pw >= 8 land
      (exists d : 0..9 | intToString(d) in pw) land
      lnot (pw = "password" lor pw = "12345678")
end

TEXT: Note that the password must be strong and secure, not weak or common.
